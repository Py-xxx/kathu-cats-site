Who We Are

Kathu Cats & Kitten Rescues is a registered NPC based in Kathu, Northern Cape. A volunteer-driven team runs the day-to-day work — no paid staff, no admin overhead. Just the work.

What We Do

We rescue abandoned and injured cats, provide medical care, sterilise to prevent future suffering, rehabilitate, and find responsible homes for cats that are ready.

Why It Matters

Kathu has a significant stray and abandoned cat problem. Without intervention, it compounds — more kittens, more suffering, more cost. We're working to change that, one cat at a time.

The Situation

The Reality on the Ground

Kathu is a mining town in the Northern Cape with a persistent stray and abandoned cat population. When families move — and in a town tied to mining contracts, they do — cats are left behind. They breed. They struggle. They show up injured, malnourished, or in desperate need of care that no-one is providing.

There are no shelters. No municipal animal welfare infrastructure. The burden falls entirely on individuals who choose to step in. The cost of a single vet visit, a sterilisation, or an emergency treatment comes directly out of pocket — or from donations that are never guaranteed and never enough.

This isn't a passion project with a comfortable budget. It's a daily effort to keep animals alive and reduce long-term suffering through sterilisation — the only intervention that actually works. We are not catching up. But we are making a dent, and we intend to keep making it.
